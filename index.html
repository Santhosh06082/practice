Meta has released a new open-source AI tool called AudioCraft. The company claims that this tool is designed to enable both professional musicians and everyday users to create audio and music from simple text prompts. AudioCraft is made up of three models: MusicGen, AudioGen, and EnCodec. MusicGen is trained using Meta's own music library and can generate music from text inputs. On the other hand, AudioGen is trained in public sound effects and can generate audio based on text inputs. Additionally, the EnCodec decoder has been improved, allowing for higher-quality music generation with fewer unwanted artifact
